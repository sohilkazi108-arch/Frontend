SHARUK KHAN

Shah Rukh Khan (SRK) Full Name: Shah Rukh Khan Born: November 2, 1965 Birthplace: New Delhi, India Profession: Actor, Producer, Entrepreneur Nicknames: King Khan, Badshah of Bollywood, SRK Early Life Shah Rukh Khan grew up in Delhi in a middle-class family. His father, Meer Taj Mohammed Khan, was involved in Indian independence activism, and his mother, Lateef Fatima, was a magistrate and social worker. Orphaned at a young age, SRK’s journey is often framed as a classic outsider story—talent, ambition, and grit over privilege. He studied economics at Hansraj College (Delhi University) and began a master’s degree in mass communication, which he later left to pursue acting. Early Career SRK began his acting career in television in the late 1980s with shows like: Fauji Circus His TV work made him a recognizable face before he moved to Mumbai with no film industry connections. Bollywood Breakthrough He made his film debut in 1992 with Deewana, which instantly launched him into stardom. Unlike typical romantic heroes of the time, SRK initially played intense and anti-hero roles in films like: Baazigar Darr Anjaam Rise to “King of Romance” By the mid-1990s, Shah Rukh Khan became Bollywood’s definitive romantic lead, especially after: Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge (1995) — one of the longest-running films in Indian cinema Iconic romantic films include: Kuch Kuch Hota Hai Mohabbatein Kabhi Khushi Kabhie Gham Veer-Zaara Devdas These films shaped modern Bollywood romance and cemented his global fan base. Reinvention & Range SRK consistently reinvented himself with complex and unconventional roles: Swades (idealistic scientist returning to India) Chak De! India (disgraced hockey coach) My Name Is Khan (man with Asperger’s syndrome) Fan, Raees, Zero After a brief career slowdown, he returned strongly with blockbuster action films, proving his longevity across generations. Producer & Businessman He co-founded Red Chillies Entertainment, one of India’s most influential production and VFX studios. He is also co-owner of: Kolkata Knight Riders (IPL cricket team) Personal Life Married to Gauri Khan since 1991 Children: Aryan, Suhana, and AbRam Khan Known for his wit, intelligence, and deeply loyal fan following Global Recognition Shah Rukh Khan is one of the most internationally recognized Indian actors. Honors include: Padma Shri (India’s civilian award) France’s Ordre des Arts et des Lettres Numerous Filmfare Awards Legacy Shah Rukh Khan’s story is often described as “the ultimate Bollywood dream”—a self-made star who rose from Delhi’s theatre and television scene to become one of the most influential entertainers in the world. He represents: Outsider success Emotional intelligence on screen The global face of Hindi cinema
